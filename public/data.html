What data is being collected?
Depending on the exact site different data may be collected. This data almost always includes Usage Data (IP Address, country of origin, User-Agent, time of access, the accessed URL). Additionally some API usage is logged. This includes any login attempts, account modifications, account deletions, and more. When using a service that requires a login the username / user id is also logged. Any errors that might occur during usage of a service may also be logged for the purpose of improving the service.

When registering the personally identifiable data that is being collected may include, but is not limited to
  • Your Email address
  • And your first & last name,
aswell as the previously mentioned Usage Data
Use of Data
The collected data is being used:

  • To provide analysis or valuable information so that we can improve the Service
  • To monitor the usage of the Service
  • To detect, prevent and address technical issues
